Jnanagni, the annual Socio-Techno-Cultural fest of Gurukula Kangri (Deemed to be University), Haridwar, is the conjugation of the best minds in the country. It is a platform for your ideas to be heard and skills to be promoted to reach the pinnacle of your mind. Demonstrate your abilities in non-technical, cultural, and sports areas.

Over the past 15 years, Jnanagni has been a stage for engineering innovations and to make raw ideas echo, setting new quality benchmarks. Jnanagni has established itself as a brand recognized by intellectuals and professionals from various fields throughout the country. It will be a three-day journey to your cerebral awakening and technical, non-technical, cultural, and athletic excellence.
